Drop your Neufreit font files here.

The CSS expects these filenames (any format works — woff2 preferred):
  Neufreit-ExtraBold.woff2  (weight 800)
  Neufreit-ExtraBold.woff
  Neufreit-ExtraBold.otf
  Neufreit-Bold.woff2       (weight 700)
  Neufreit-Bold.woff
  Neufreit-Bold.otf
  Neufreit-Regular.woff2    (weight 400)
  Neufreit-Regular.woff
  Neufreit-Regular.otf

If your files have different names, update the @font-face src paths
near the top of index.html to match.

Until the files are added, the form falls back to Playfair Display.
